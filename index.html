Cat Care Basics

  • Nutrition: Feed balanced food appropriate for age and health.
  • Health: Regular vet check-ups and vaccinations.
  • Environment: Provide scratching posts, toys, and safe hiding spots.
  • Grooming: Brush as needed and maintain dental care.
